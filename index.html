NASA's EPIC Camera

The EPIC, in laymans terms is a telescope with a camera inside that is attached aboard NOAA's DSCVR spacecraft. This site offers a look back at our planet through the lens of the EPIC over the last few years (excluding 2019, for some reason that information is not available) during the date of October 27th. Click on the dropdown button below to access a list of available dates to choose from. You will be redirected to images with more information. For more information on the EPIC and it's API please visit the EPIC website here.
